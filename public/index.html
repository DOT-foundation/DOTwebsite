Why help us?

Donors of Tomorrow is a non-profit organization formed by devoted students looking to make a change for the better of the community. We aim to make sure others can find a place in this community, no matter who they are or where they come from.

Our Mission

The DOT Foundation was founded by students dedicated to supporting the underprivileged, homeless, children, and disabled individuals in our community. Our mission is to provide essentials, raise funds, and be the beacon of hope on their path to a brighter future.
